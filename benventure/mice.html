An Assembly of Mice

The tunnel enlarged slightly and Octavian found himself in a small room. Gathered all around was a throng of mice, only slightly larger than usual. Upon seeing him, one of the mice turned and exclaimed "Well what's this then?"

"Hello, my name's Octavian" he replied. "I'm looking for some carrots of mine that went missing."

"Some sob story that is" another mouse chimed in. "Just like you humans, always going off on some 'quest'. You think you're so self-important. You just look like a big naked badger to me."

Slightly taken aback, Octavian asked "pardon my boldness, but perhaps you might help me find the one that took them?"

"Maybe we'll help you" replied another mouse. "But you've got to impress us first. Put on a little show and if we like it, we'll point you in the right direction. Dance for us, monkey!"
