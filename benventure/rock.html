Rock Wall

Octavian came upon a rock wall that covered the entire cross-section of the tunnel. He stopped to collect his thoughts.

Feeling around the wall, Octavian realized that one of the rocks was a little loose. Based on the arrangement of the stones, he figured that a well-placed shove might trigger a chain reaction and bring the whole thing down. The only thing he wasn't so sure about was what he might find on the other side.

But with no other path forward, he decided that dislodging the rock was worth a shot.
