Octavian and the Carrots

It was Christmas time, and Octavian the erstwhile bard had been invited to a big Christmas dinner by his friends. He was tasked with bringing carrots for the meal.

It had been good weather this year--not too hot, not too cold--and Octavian's garden had done quite well in response. He had enjoyed many a long summer day working in the soil and tending to the crops.

In light of this opportunity to share the fruits of his labor, Octavian went out to the root cellar to collect a handful of carrots. But when he opened the door and looked in, he saw a large hole in the place where he carrots had been.

"Quite odd" he thought to himself. But there was only one way to get to the bottom of this...
