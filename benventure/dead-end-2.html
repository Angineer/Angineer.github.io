Just a Note

Octavian looked around, but the only thing he saw was a note on the ground. It read:

To find the thing you seek
Three clues you must find
Simply view the correct sources
And read between the lines

The three clues together
Will make a simple phrase
Just put it in your path
And upon the solution you will gaze

Below the words, there were three pictures in this order:

A sleeping person, a mouse, and a well.
