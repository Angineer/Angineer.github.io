A Cricket

In the next section of tunnel, Octavian came across a little cricket. Upon seeing Octavian, the cricket seemed to perk up a little.

"Hello" said the insect. "My name is Jim. I seem to have gotten myself a little lost down here and perhaps you could help me."

"Alright" said Octavian. "I'll give it a shot."

"I'm confident that I can find my way out eventually" explained Jim. "But the real question is about sustenance. You see, I have a few seeds and some grass trimmings here with me. I'm getting quite hungry, but I don't know how much further I will have to go before I reach the surface."

"So would it be better for me to wait and stretch my rations longer, or am I safe to consume them now?"
